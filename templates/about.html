About FinSaathi

A safe space to learn the stock market — using real prices, virtual money, and zero risk.

🎯 What this is

FinSaathi is a virtual stock trading platform built to help people learn investing without the fear of losing real money. You get ₹1,00,000 of virtual capital, and you can buy and sell real NSE-listed stocks using live market prices — exactly like a real brokerage app, just without real money on the line.

Think of it as a flight simulator for investing: the controls are real, the market is real, but nothing crashes for real.

💬 Why I built it

Most of us were never taught how to invest — not in school, not at home. Many people assume the stock market is gambling, simply because no one ever explained the basics in a low-pressure way.

I built FinSaathi for our family — a small, private space where everyone, regardless of experience, can practice investing, make mistakes that don't cost anything, and slowly build the confidence to invest for real one day.

🚀 How it works

  1. 1
    Register with your name and a 4-digit PIN — no email, no real money, no risk.
  2. 2
    Receive ₹1,00,000 in virtual capital instantly.
  3. 3
    Search and trade real NSE stocks using live prices — Market, Limit, or Stop-loss orders.
  4. 4
    Track your portfolio, P&L, and compete on the family leaderboard.
  5. 5
    Earn XP, level up, and build daily streaks as you learn and trade.

🛠️ Built with

FinSaathi is built end-to-end as a personal learning project — Python and Flask for the backend, live NSE data via Yahoo Finance, a Supabase cloud database so your progress is never lost, and hosted on Render.

⚠️ Important disclaimer

FinSaathi uses 100% virtual money — no real funds are ever involved, deposited, or at risk. This platform is built purely for educational purposes and is not a registered broker, investment advisor, or financial institution.

Nothing on FinSaathi constitutes real financial or investment advice. Stock prices shown are real and sourced from public market data, but all trading activity here is simulated. Please consult a SEBI-registered advisor before making any real investment decisions.
